Radio Frequency Energy Harvesting

There are three segments

  1. Antenna
  2. Rectifier
  3. Rectenna

Antenna

The first part is the receiving antenna. It operates at 2.45 GHz and receives the ambient energy signals.

Rectifier

The second part is the rectifying unit, it converts the received ambient RF signal into usable DC signal with the help of diodes and capacitors.

Rectenna

This shows the integration of both the rectifier and antenna unit which gives rise to the Rectenna.
